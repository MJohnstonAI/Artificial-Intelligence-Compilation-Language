# Sponsorship and Research Support

AICL is an open research and development initiative.

The project welcomes:
- academic collaboration
- formal methods and verification support
- compiler and runtime implementation work
- ontology and WKG research
- multimodal systems research
- agent workflow experimentation
- grants, sponsorships, and technical partnerships

## Principles

Support for AICL does **not** purchase:
- authorship of the original language concept
- ownership of the canonical specification
- editorial control over the core language vision

Support may help accelerate:
- research and formalization
- public documentation
- reference implementations
- demo systems
- educational materials
- open-source community growth
